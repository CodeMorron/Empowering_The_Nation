EMPOWERING THE NATION

WHO ARE WE

Empowering the Nation, founded in 2018 by Precious Radebe, is transforming the lives of domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Through dynamic six-week courses and six-month learnerships, we equip individuals with practical, marketable skills. Our mission is to elevate career opportunities, increase earning potential, and inspire entrepreneurship. Join us in empowering the workforce and building a brighter, more prosperous community!
