Welcome to Empowering The Nation

Building Careers, Empowering Communities

Precious Radebe grew up in a humble neighborhood in Johannesburg, where she witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by domestic workers and gardeners striving for a better life. Her mother, a domestic worker herself, worked tirelessly to provide for the family, often sacrificing her dreams for the sake of her children. Inspired by her mother’s resilience and determination, Precious vowed to create a brighter future for those in her community. After completing her education, Precious worked in various roles within community development and social work, always with a focus on uplifting others. However, she felt a calling to do more, to empower individuals with the skills needed to enhance their livelihoods and break the cycle of poverty. In 2018, fueled by her passion and vision, Precious founded Empowering the Nation. She set out to offer practical training programs designed to equip domestic workers and gardeners with marketable skills, helping them secure better job opportunities and become financially independent. Under her leadership, Empowering the Nation has grown into a vibrant community hub, where individuals not only learn valuable skills but also gain confidence, forge connections, and rediscover their potential. Precious’s journey is a testament to the power of resilience, compassion, and the belief that with the right support and education, anyone can change their story and shape their future.
